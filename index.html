About Kasey's Kreations

I was born in Ecuador, but I moved and grew up in Charlotte, North Carolina since I was seven years old. I graduated from both high school and college in Charlotte. I attended Central Piedmont Community College and graduated from UNC-Charlotte in 2018 with a B.S in Computer Science. I've been working at AvidXchange in Charlotte since September 2018 as Test Automation.

In my spare time, I enjoy learning web development, whether that be by reading/watching tutorials or creating personal side projects. Currently, I am following the front end path by first learning HTML and CSS. Next step would be learning JavaScript, and eventually a front end framework.

When I'm not focused on web development, I enjoy watching netflix, movies and video games, going for jogs and playing basketball.
